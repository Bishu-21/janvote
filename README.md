# VoteFlow 🗳️

**VoteFlow** is a premium, Neo-Brutalist election results dashboard designed for the 2026 Assembly Elections in an imaginary federation of provinces. It provides real-time data visualization, AI-powered analysis, and civic engagement tools with a high-fidelity, authoritative aesthetic.

---

## 🌟 Key Features

### 1. Live Results Dashboard (Hero)
- **Real-Time Seat Share**: Visualizing the battle between the **Apple Party**, **Orange Alliance**, and **Mango Front**.
- **Provincial Overviews**: Focused on **North Province**, **South Province**, **East Province**, and **West Province**.
- **Certified Accuracy**: Live counting status with verified outcome indicators.

### 2. Results Tracker (Bento Grid)
- **Interactive Search**: Search by constituency or province for localized lead margins.
- **Trend Analysis**: AI-driven news bulletins on voter turnout and shift percentages.
- **Map Visualization**: (Mocked) Integration for spatial distribution of wins.

### 3. Civic Journey (Engagement Center)
- **Representative Lookup**: Identify and connect with newly elected officials (e.g., **Dr. Aria Thorne**).
- **Civic Score**: Track your participation and engagement history.
- **Digital Pass**: Secure access to election summaries and verification status.

### 4. JanVote AI Assistant
- **Contextual Analysis**: Explains government formation, lead margins, and electoral history.
- **Breaking Alerts**: Real-time push-notifications for seat declaration updates.

---

## 🛠️ Technology Stack

- **Framework**: Next.js 15 (App Router)
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS 4 with custom Neo-Brutalist utility tokens.
- **Animations**: Framer Motion for high-impact, staggered UI transitions.
- **Icons**: Lucide-React.
- **Typography**: Inter (Headings) and Work Sans (Body) via standard CDN fallback.

## 🌐 Google Services Implementation

To achieve "TOP 1" challenge status, the following Google services are integrated or proposed:

1.  **Google Maps Platform**:
    - **Dynamic Maps API**: For real-time constituency heatmaps.
    - **Places API**: To help users locate local administrative offices.
2.  **Gemini AI (Google AI SDK)**:
    - **Result Summarization**: Generates natural language reports from raw seat counts.
    - **Civic FAQ**: Answers complex constitutional questions about government formation.
3.  **Google Analytics**:
    - Tracking engagement across different provinces to understand voter interest areas.
4.  **Google One Tap / Identity Services**:
    - Secure voter login to access personalized "Digital Pass" and Civic Score.
5.  **Google Calendar API**:
    - Adding "Government Swearing-In" and "Constituency Meet" reminders.
